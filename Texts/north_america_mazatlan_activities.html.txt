 Activities  Mazatl n is world famous for its sport fishing - especially for marlin, swordfish, sailfish, tuna and dorado (dolphinfish). There are plenty of fishing operators, all of whom should offer tag and release options. Book ahead for the winter high season.   Water sports, including scuba diving, water-skiing, sailing, parasailing and boogie boarding are all on offer at the Aqua Sports Centre at El Cid Mega Resort. The best surfing is at Punta Camar n and 'Cannons', off the point near the old fort on Paseo Olas Altas.
