 Activities  Bosnia-Hercegovina's ample forests, streams and mountains provide the potential for some serious outdoor adventuring. Hunting, hiking, rafting and fishing opportunities abound, as do potentially tamer pursuits at any of the country's ski resorts and health spas. Though plans are underway to develop some of these activities into regular tourist draws, the specter of the nation's bloody past is proving to be a formidable obstacle to that goal. So will the myriad mines and booby traps planted throughout the country's forests, which still take a heavy toll on wildlife.
