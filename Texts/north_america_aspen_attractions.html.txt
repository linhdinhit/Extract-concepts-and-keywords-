 Attractions  From 'blue cruisers' to 'double-blacks,' Ajax's slopes boast a variety of terrain type, though don't expect anything too green and easy. The globe's ace skiers have zoomed through here, after all, and they aren't going to bother with the bunnies. On its palate are 76 trails wending through 673 acres (272 hectares). Snowboarders, don't fret: since 2001, you're allowed in, too.  After the snow has melted - or before it has started its fall - the Silver Queen Gondola carries passengers from the center of town to the top of the mountain, covering a vertical expanse of 3000ft (914m). Up top, guides from the Aspen Center of Environmental Studies await to escort you around the natural setting.
