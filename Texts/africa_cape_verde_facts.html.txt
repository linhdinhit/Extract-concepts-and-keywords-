 Facts for the Traveler   Visas: all visitors need visasHealth risks: yellow feverTime: GMT/UTC minus 1 hourElectricity: 220V, 50HzWeights & measures: metric When to Go  The best time to visit Cape Verde is from August to October, when the weather is pleasantly warm, though the winds are pretty stiff year round, so bring a windcheater. The rest of the year is much cooler.     Events  Cape Verde has one of Africa's most vibrant Carnival celebrations. It's the country's major party, with street parades in February in Praia and Mindelo. On S o Tiago and Fogo, Tabanka is celebrated in May and June and marked by music and abstinence. Each island also has its own festival, with the party going on for about a week.
