 Activities  The Gauja valley is a winter sports centre, and has a bobsleigh run at Sigulda and a ski jump at Valmiera. Riga has an outdoor ice skating rink that opens when temperatures fall below -3 C (27 F).   Canoeing is very popular on the Gauja, Salaca and Abava rivers and in the Latgale lakes region. Gauja National Park can be visited on horse-riding excursions arranged in Sigulda. Private companies offer guided bird-watching trips from Baldone, about 20km (12mi) south of Riga.
