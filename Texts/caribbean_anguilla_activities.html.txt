 Activities  Anguilla has plenty of beautiful beaches should the mood strike you to take a dip or lather yourself with tanning butter. Sandy Ground has calm turquoise waters, as do the impressive sweeps at Shoal Bay East and Rendezvous Bay. Other notable beaches include Meads Bay and Shoal Bay West. Top honors in the Valley area go to Crocus Bay.  Anguilla has clear water suitable for diving and snorkeling. Since the mid-1980s a number of ships have been deliberately sunk in Anguillian waters to create dive sites; they lie on sandy bottoms at depths of 35-75ft (11-23m). Nearby islets popular for diving include Prickly Pear Cays and Sandy Islands.
