 Facts for the Traveler   Visas: Everyone except French nationals needs a visa. They cost around US$20 and are valid for one month. Events  Friday is a holiday for offices and government institutions. Djibouti also observes New Year's Day (1 January), Labour Day (1 May), Independence Day (27 June), Christmas Day (25 December) and various Islamic holidays whose dates are variable.
