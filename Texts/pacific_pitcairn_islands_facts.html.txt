 Facts for the Traveler   Visas: None needed if you make a brief visit from a passing ship. If you want to stay longer you have to apply through the British Consulate General in Auckland. Allow plenty of time as it will take about six months for the application to be considered.Health risks: NoneTime: GMT/UTC minus 8.5 hoursWeights & measures: Metric When to Go  July and August are probably the best months to go, but unless you get here aboard your own yacht, your choice is limited by the timetables of the passenger ships that call in.     Events   Church on Saturdays is the most regular event on the island, and birthdays are usually good excuses for a get together and celebration. Each year on 23 January the Bounty's demise is celebrated by towing a burning model of the ship across Bounty Bay.
